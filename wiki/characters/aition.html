Development

temp

When growing up in Archaina, Aition was not fond of living amongst others. He found most people petty and superficial, bickering over insecurities and spreading rumours. He wants no part of this and spends the majority of his day avoiding stepping on the toes of others. This often leads him to pick the most frictionless course of action if it means less hassle and energy being spent over meaningless quarrels. In this way, Aition is often perceived as self centred due to his lack of interest in others... because he is. But not in a dubious way where he will gloat or step on others to get ahead. He knows his place in the world, but just doesn't have much concern or respect for those around him. He is closed off from others, shrouded behind a protective veil.
This attitude begins to shift as he spends time on the island, and grows an appreciation for cooperation and community. This development culminates with the adoption of {young child} as he finally decides to open his life up to others and discovers the value of service and family.
